Our Constitution

MEMBERSHIP
i. New members who wish to be part of the EAGLES CLUB will pay Ksh 100 for registration. None refundable
ii. Any member who wish to quit the Club will be paid 85% of his/her contribution plus the profit
iii. In case a new member who is not physically available within K.U and wants to join the club, he/she should have a representative and contribute through him/her.

ATTENDANCE
Every registered member is encouraged not to miss any meeting.
All members to attend the meeting which be held on Monday of the first and the last week of every month from 5:10pm.
VENUE: NYAYO 4 COMMON ROOM
NOTE:  Late attendance will be charged at Ksh10/= which will be paid on arrival.
 Missing the meeting without a valid apology will be charged at Ksh 50/= to be paid on the next meeting.

CONTRIBUTION o All members to contribute at least Ksh 500/= every month towards the club.
o The minimum amount to contribute is Ksh 500/= and no maximum amount.
o The Deadline for the contribution is date 5th of the next month for the previous month.
NOTE:  Any late payment will be charged at a rate of 10% of one’s contribution every day.

LOANS
o All members are eligible for the loan provided he/she contributes to the group.
o A member can borrow up to 90% of his/her contribution with a simple interest of 10% per month.
o A non-member can also access our loans only with a member acting as a guarantor and can borrow not exceeding 90% of his/her guarantor with an interest rate of 15% per month.
NOTE:
 Any late payment of the loan will be charged a fine 2% per day for all members.
 A non-member will be charged a fine of 3% per day for late payment.

PROFIT SHARING
Profit made by the club is shared in the ratio according to one’s contribution to the club.
